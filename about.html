About us

We are an independent Software development and Videogame Design studio.
Starting in early 2018 by a small collective of family and friends, this project started out by developing small and private games before turning to commercial development.

The studio name was inspired by our early projects where we always seek to create highly performing task automated software applications commonly known in the videogame realm as 'BOT'.
